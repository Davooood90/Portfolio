Course Companion (CoCo) began as an idea in 2021, but it wasn't until the summer of 2022 that the project truly came to life. What started as a simple spreadsheet has since evolved into a fully functional web app.

In the early stages, I served as both a project manager and developer. However, as we brought on more team members—many with greater programming expertise—I shifted my focus solely to project management.

Currently, CoCo is tailored specifically to my old high school, but our vision is much broader. Teams at UofA and UBC are working to expand its compatibility to their respective institutions, with the goal of reaching more students.

Languages: HTML • CSS • TypeScript • JavaScript

The launch of ChatGPT at the end of 2022 sparked a wave of concern, with many news outlets predicting the "start of an AI takeover." But we believe that responsibility rests with us, humanity. When used thoughtfully, these tools hold immense potential to uplift, inspire, and create.

ValeNLP is a project that turns short compliments into poetry — custom-tailored messages of encouragement and appreciation to students, who may otherwise forget that they are appreciated and consequently feel isolated or disconnected from their communities.

With the help of 45 dedicated volunteers, we delivered cards to everyone in our school—students, teachers, and staff alike. In collaboration with AI, nearly 80,000 words were written, each one crafted from custom data to reflect the individuality of each person.
